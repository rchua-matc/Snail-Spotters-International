Become A Spotter

Interested in joining Snail Spotters International? Fill out the form below and you'll be contacted by the chapter of your choice with more information about benefits, fees, and the initiation ritual.

Members of Snail Spotters International get exclusive access to monthly issues of our newsletter, Spotted!. New members automatically receive their choice of Snail Spotters International introductory merchandise (iron-on logo patch, snail keychain, "Snail Spotter" vanity license plate frame, or coffee mug) as well as the ability to recruit new distributors to our merchandising network.

Upon joining, you'll receive a unique referral code to give when recruiting new Snail Spotters affiliates. You earn a portrion of each sale made by recruits downline of you while paying a small annual fee based on performance.

As a member, you'll also be able to participate in your chapter's educational seminars, where you can help spread the word about the Iowa Pleistocene snail and the benefits of becoming a Snail Spotter affiliate member. We also recommend hosting "snail parties" for friends and family to learn more about the endangered snail. Snail parties are also a great place to sell the Snail Spotters International Affiliate Starter Kit™ to create new levels of our marketing team.
